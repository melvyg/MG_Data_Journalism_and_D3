Correlation Between Salary and Education Attainment

There appears to be a positive correlation with having a graduate degree or higher and having a salary of $50,000+ (~0.53). Similarly, The correlation of having a bachelors degree and a salary of $50,000+ is nearly the same as having a graduate degree or higher. However, having a high school degree or lower and a $50,000+ salary has a negative correlation of about ~ -0.35.
